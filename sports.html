Sports Department

The Sports Department at AKYSB Ali Jiwani is dedicated to fostering teamwork, discipline, and physical fitness among youth. Our goal is to create a platform where young individuals can excel in sports, build character, and develop leadership skills. Through various programs and events, we aim to inspire a lifelong love for sports and healthy living.
