Arts Department

The Arts Department at AKYSB Ali Jiwani is dedicated to fostering creativity, self-expression, and innovation among youth. Our goal is to provide a platform where young individuals can explore their artistic talents, develop new skills, and showcase their work. Through workshops, exhibitions, and community projects, we aim to inspire a lifelong appreciation for the arts.
